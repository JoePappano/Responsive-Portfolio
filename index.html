About Me

Hi! My name is Joe Pappano and welcome to my website! The page you're on is an about me section, so let me introduce myself:


I grew up outside of Philadelphia, PA and studied at West Chester East High School. I was a multi-sport athlete and liked to spend time with friends outside on adventures. After graduation, I went to the University of Texas at Austin, where I majored in International Relations and Global Studies. After graduation from UT, I went to work for Total Quality Logistics, a Cincinnati-based logistics firm. Next, I worked for a tech start-up called OutboundEngine, then I got another job at an Austin-based logistics firm, called Arrive Logistics, and this is where I currently work.


I love puzzles and building things, so a very close friend recommended I spend some time online learning how to code, I loved it and I decided to learn more. And now I am here at the University of Texas Coding Boot Camp writing this to you.
